Make your own Phaser game:

  1. Start making you game by forking this blank game.
  2. Begin with Scene1.js for your first scene.
  3. You can add more scenes by copying and pasting Scene2.js.
  4. Use the share button in the top right corner to share the game with your teammates, so can work on it at the same time (multiplayer).
  5. Good luck and have fun on your game making adventure.
